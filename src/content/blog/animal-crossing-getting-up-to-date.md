Its been a while, so some of you been wandering how are things going on my little Animal Crossing Town.

Well....

Through out the Month of August I found out there were doing Morning excercises between 6-7 Am. I joined some of the villagers, and tried my best to follow the instructor and failling.

Turns out if I attended the class 14 times I get a prize! I confess I cheated on this one by changing the ingame clock since Im not that much of a morning bird.

![all the villager doing morning excerises](/images/2023/animalcrossingworkout.png#original)

![the turtle mayor of animal crossing saying: But somehow, I never though it´d be mcuh fun for you to come here to excercise every morning, sprout!](/images/2023/animalcrossingtortimerworkout.png#original)

I also managed to finish my first house payment, painted it purple only for the villager start trying to randomly paint my roof. I still haven´t forgiven Mathilda for painted it green.

Also as a result of my shopping Nook´s got an upgrade twice. No wonder a shabby shack its is now a proper modern store with extra space for more items for me to buy. Nice.

![a house with a purple roof](/images/2023/animalcrossing%20housepurple.png#original)

![a white modern version of the nook store](/images/2023/animalcrossingtomnookstore.png#original)

![a modern white shop video with a sign that says nookway](/images/2023/animalcrossingnookway.png#original)


![the nook store on the inside offering several items for sale](/images/2023/animalcrossingnookwayinside.png#original)


This town seems to be gainning a reputation as I´ve got a bunch of wacky new visitors too. There was this seagull who kept showing up on the beach and gave me a gift for helping him.

![a seagull sailor asleep on a beach](/images/2023/animalcrossingseagullbeach.png#original)


Got a visit from Wendell too, he keeps asking me for food. i gave him fruit didn´t work. However he does seems partial to fish. And I got an exculsive wallpaper out of him.

![a warlus called wendell saying this is my favorite fish](/images/2023/animalcrossingwalruspainter.png#original)


There is also the turnip lady, the games equivalent of stocks. For some reason I keep missing her. She only shows up at a specific time, you see.


![a warthog lady selling turnips](/images/2023/turniplady.png#original)

I also got another camper, which is a chicken called Hector, here for the sights.

![Hecktor the blue chicken on a tent saying: My name´s Hector, but I´m also known in some circles as "The Mad Hiker" or King Camper!](/images/2023/hectorthechicken.png#original)

Much to my suprise there several new people just moving in, two felines (Rolf the Tiger and Olivia the Cat),Daisy the dog who is a cutie, Dizzy the elephant and of course Hazel the Squirel.

Except of all of them Hazel stay wasn´t for long. I´d figure that dizzy would be the one to leave since it´s been a while we last talked. But just goes to show you control villager. Seriously they will leave at time and you won´t even know why.


![hazel a brown squirell saying: Oh! hi there! good morning.](/images/2023/animalcrossingsquirll.png#original)

![rolf the white tiger saying: Hey! Who are you? I certainly don´t know you! Hmmm... ghastly??? Nice name, grrrolf.](/images/2023/rolfthetiger.png#original)

![Pate a blue duck with rosy cheecks saying<.> Hey! Don´t be so nervous when you´re trying to make a good first impression. Doe](/images/2023/animalcrossingblueduck.png#original)

![Olivia the white cat saying: Oh, no! Not another fan, purrr! Curse my movie star](/images/2023/animalcrossingwhitekitty.png#original)

![Daisy the white Dog. Hi! Nice to meet you. I haven´t lived in batvale too long, but as far as I know, it´s a pretty nice place](/images/2023/cutewhitedog.jpeg#original)

To end this post I managed to go to Nook´s Raffle constest. I save enough tickets to hopefully get some cool prizes, and I got one of the coolest items in the game:

![the player character acessing the donkey kong console](/images/2023/animalcrossinadonkeykongconsole.jpeg#original)


![menu for the donkey kong game](/images/2023/animalcrossingdonkeykongmenu.jpeg#original)


A donkey kong console game! Not only that it´s actually playable ingame. A game within a game, I would say. It´s a shame they decided to remove this feature in future version of AC. It was really one of the more unique features of the game.

My theory is that they decided to remove it as they started reselling classic games through the virtual console on the wii, and 3ds, hence it never made a comeback.

Still it was a fun discovery.

I haven´t managed to play every day, but let´s see what September will bring, specially since there is a speciall even on the 29th.
