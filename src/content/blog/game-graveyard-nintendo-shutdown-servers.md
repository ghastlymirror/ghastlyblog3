Sad news for those who are still attached to our 3DS and Wii console. In Early April next year Nintendo plans on retiring their online services, meanwhile certain things like be able to transfer Pokemon from older games will be practically impossible.

But its not all bleak as there is a way to preserve them.

Meet the [Pretendo.Network project](https://pretendo.network/). Created way before news of the shuttdown, they seek to maintain online functionality, way after the original services have long been gone.

It will provide users with custom servers for your multiplayer needs, they are even able to bring back the Miiverse, which a very a beloved social App among Nintendo Fans.

All you need is a modded 3DS/Wii U. (which if you haven´t done already, i suggest you do so asap, I even wrote a [post about it](/blog/first-foray-into-3ds-hacking))

Of course Pretendo provides a series of instructions for installation.

But if for some reason you don´t have acess to a console, or you just prefer to game on your PC, the CEMU emulator is compatible with this project. As for the 3DS sadly there is no emulator compatiability, yet.

In the mean time make to enjoy the official multiplayer servers while you still can as it might be a while, before we them working again
