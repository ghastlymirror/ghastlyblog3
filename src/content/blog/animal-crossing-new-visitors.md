More new happenings at Batvale!

I started to fall into a decent rythm in town, I collect bugs or fruit or fossils, help my fellow villagers than sell all that extra stuff to Nook.

First things first I finally got the fishing rod! Yes I can now go fishing and start working on completing the aquarium section of the Museum. I even wore a special outfit for the ocasion.



![the player catching a fish](/images/2023/animalcrossingkrillfish.png#original "Jambe even congratulated me on my first catch"
) 



We also got two brand new faces, who are both horses. Winnie seems a bit paranoid but Ed now I really love Ed, his sense of style is iconic, I really love his design and also his house.

![Ed a blue feminine horse with a blond mane, pink eyeshadow and a purple and pink dress](/images/2023/horsevillager.png#original)



![a house fulll of checkboard furniture](/images/2023/animalcrossingcheckboardhouse.png#original "Cool House")


I hope we become close friends.

Anyway, As I walk I sometimes check with the Cooper to see if there ´s anything interesting going on and wouldn´t you know it. Kathrina is in town.

![an excotic looking tent in the middle of the town, it has a read color with some yellow decorations](/images/2023/animalcrossingseertent.png#original)


![a black cat who is a fortune telling next to a crystal ball](/images/2023/animalcrossingseer.png#original)


See does what she alwasy does I pay her to have my fortune read. Wonder what that´s is all about.

He also at one point kept telling me there wre going to have an event about Car safety, then he complained about some jackass doing some illegally parking

Turns out is famous Celebrity and Animal Crossing Own Fashion Icon, Gracie! The one Mathilda my archnemesis keeps gushing about.

She asked me to clean her car in return I would get a special clothing from her. Apperantly Prison chick is in right now.

![a fancy looking giraffe next to a luxurios red car with leopard print seats](/images/2023/animalcrossinggiraffe.png#original)

![walking on a museum exibit surrounded by trees](/images/2023/animalcrossingbugmuseum.png#original)


12th August turns around and turns out there´s a Meteor shower! (Funny enought there was also one happening in real life). Everyone was at the lake to see it. It was Nice.

![good evening. the summer meteor shower reachers its peak tonight! come watch it by the lake!](/images/2023/animalcrossingmeteorshower.png#original)

What I didn´t expect was seeing Chevre and Mathilda together? What do you know opposites do attract.

![the player, mathilda the black kangaroo and chevre the white goat together near the lake](/images/2023/animalcrossingmeteorlake.png#original)

![chevre saying: watching the meteor shower together, la baa!](/images/2023/animalcrossingchevremeteor.png#original)


This event must be really popular because it also attracted new visitor in town, who came to camp out just so he could spend the night looking at the stars.

![pierce the blue eagle saying: If you can guess which animals I took pictures of when I was camping, you get a prize!](/images/2023/animalcrossingeaglecamp.png#original)


Turns out he´s also a bit of a photographer so we hit it off right a way. He decided to test my eye for photography so he challenged me to figured which one of these photos were his.

I won both time. Got a cooler and a mountain bike out of it, nice.

And that is basically it for now.

Stay tune on my next adventures in Batvale!
