As I´m writing this it is the last day of August and with it the end of of Blaugust. So how did I went? Well for one thing I ended up uploading less posts than last year.

It happened for two reasons. For one, I was having some problems in trying to upload some posts. Last year I had already had a few that were already scheduled so it made my job easier.

I was unable to do so this year.

Another is more of the life got in the way. This last week I got really sick, which kinda sucks since its still summer, and also I had to rest for a week.

That being said even thought I did not suceed in fufilling my post goals, I still mannaged to upload 11 including this one, which is good enough for me. And I at least managed to keep blogging for a second year and I hope they will still be many more.

I was not seeking any theme in practicular except talking about the things that I just got interested lately, so far I started an Animal Crossing playthrough, as well got into a new mmo, book series.

I even learned how to hack a 3DS console, since the eShop is dead and I can paly all the 3DS and even some old games to by hearts content.

Also, despite not writting as much as I wanted, I still got something out of it. For one I got more eyes on my blog, which was the same as last year. and found some new bloggers as well.

Althought I´m doing this more as a way to motivate myself to write instead of just self promotion, it is nice to see the fruits of my own effort. And good to know there are people out there still insterested in traditional blogging.

Do I plan on doing this event next year? Yes. I will even take my main failures as a lesson and come even better prepared in the future.

And that is it for this year, I wil, now return to my old schedule which is just posting weekly or more, depending on my mood.

That being said if you have been following me so far, thank you. I also enjoy reading some of the comments yall sent me.

I also want send a huge shoutout to all my fellow Blaugust participants, I hope you had fun and congrats as I know finding inspiration to write can´t be easy and I wish yall a good luck on your blogging journey.

See ya in September!
